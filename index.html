Wellbore Stability

Navigating the
Precise Drilling Window

APWD-integrated pressure tuning keeps every foot of hole inside the window — automatically.

Left Boundary: Pore Pressure
Drilling below pore pressure triggers influx and kick events. TTDownhole's real-time gain/loss detection responds before the driller feels it.
Managed ECD Curve
Live annular pressure data drives micro-adjustments via automated choke control — maintaining wellbore stability in real time, foot by foot.
Right Boundary: Fracture Gradient
Exceeding fracture gradient causes lost returns and fluid loss. TTDownhole's hydraulic model is updated continuously to maintain safe standoff.
